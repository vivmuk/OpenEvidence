## 1. AI Clinical Search & Evidence-Based Knowledge Platforms

### OpenEvidence
- **URL:** https://www.openevidence.com
- **Category:** AI clinical search engine / evidence platform
- **Target users:** Physicians, nurses, pharmacists, NPs, PAs
- **Key features:** AI-powered medical search built on peer-reviewed literature from NEJM, JAMA, Cochrane, NCCN, Wiley. Free for verified U.S. HCPs. 760K+ registered physicians, 18M+ clinical consultations/month. DeepConsult AI agent. $12B valuation (Series D, Jan 2026). Official AI partnerships with NEJM, JAMA, NCCN, Cochrane.
- **Geographic reach:** United States (withdrew from EU/UK Apr 2025 due to AI Act)
- **Notable:** Series D $250M at $12B valuation (Thrive + DST, Jan 2026); 1M consultations/day milestone (Mar 2026)

### AMBOSS
- **URL:** https://www.amboss.com
- **Category:** Medical knowledge platform with AI Mode
- **Target users:** Physicians, residents, medical students
- **Key features:** AI Mode (LiSA 1.0) ranked #1 in Stanford–Harvard NOHARM Study for clinical care safety. QBank + clinical reference + educator support. 5-day free trial; institutional plans. Used by Yale, Weill Cornell, NYU, HCA.
- **Geographic reach:** Global (multi-language, US/EU focus)

### UpToDate (Wolters Kluwer)
- **URL:** https://www.uptodate.com
- **Category:** Evidence-based clinical reference / point-of-care
- **Target users:** Physicians, nurses, pharmacists, residents, medical students
- **Key features:** 7,600+ physician-authored topics across 25+ specialties; drug monographs (Lexidrug); drug-drug interaction checker; medical calculators; CME credit; mobile + offline. Now integrating AI capabilities.
- **Geographic reach:** Global (available worldwide via subscription); free access programs in Norway, NZ, Haiti, Nepal; Ariadne Labs "Better Evidence" program for developing countries
- **Notable:** Launched 1992; the most widely used point-of-care clinical reference globally; ~$579/year physician subscription (US)

### DynaMed / DynaMedex (EBSCO)
- **URL:** https://www.dynamed.com
- **Category:** Evidence-based clinical decision support / point-of-care
- **Target users:** Physicians, nurses, pharmacists, PAs, NPs
- **Key features:** Dyna AI for clinical answers; daily-updated summaries; drug interaction alerts; clinical calculators; guideline summaries; chemo regimens; CME. Integrated with Micromedex as "DynaMedex."
- **Geographic reach:** Global (institutional subscriptions)
- **Notable:** 2024 Best in KLAS for Clinical Decision Support

### BMJ Best Practice
- **URL:** https://bestpractice.bmj.com
- **Category:** Evidence-based clinical decision support
- **Target users:** Physicians, nurses, medical students, pharmacists
- **Key features:** Step-by-step diagnosis and treatment guidance; condition monographs; drug information; CME; mobile app; regular evidence updates
- **Geographic reach:** Global; included in NSW Health Clinical Information Access Portal (Australia); free access via Norwegian Electronic Health Library
- **Notable:** Published by BMJ; launched 2009; maximum scores for editorial quality in JMIR (2016)

### ClinicalKey / ClinicalKey AI (Elsevier)
- **URL:** https://www.clinicalkey.com
- **Category:** Medical knowledge platform / AI-powered clinical reference
- **Target users:** Physicians, nurses, researchers, medical students
- **Key features:** Full-text access to Elsevier medical books, journals, videos, images; drug monographs; clinical overviews; AI-powered answers from evidence-based content; EHR integration
- **Geographic reach:** Global (institutional subscriptions)

### AccessMedicine (McGraw-Hill)

- **Target users:** Medical students, residents, physicians
- **Key features:** Harrison's Principles of Internal Medicine; Current Medical Diagnosis & Treatment; Goodman & Gilman's pharmacology; study tools; case files; diagnostic tools; board review

- **URL:** https://www.verahealth.ai
- **Category:** AI clinical search engine / evidence platform
- **Target users:** Physicians, nurses, students globally
- **Key features:** AI-powered search over 60M+ peer-reviewed papers. 900+ clinical calculators. Multilingual. ACEP partnership for emergency medicine. CME credits. No pharma ads. HIPAA + GDPR compliant. Free globally — no NPI requirement. Scores 84.9% NEJM-AI, 97.5% USMLE.

- **URL:** https://www.heidihealth.com
- **Category:** AI clinical search + ambient documentation

Heidi Comms for patient calls/bookings/reminders. Heidi Remote wearable mic. 110+ languages, 116 countries, 200+ specialties. 2.4M patient visits/week.
